Question: Give all driver names
SQL: SELECT DISTINCT forename, surname FROM drivers;

Question: Give me all male patients
SQL: SELECT * FROM patient WHERE sex = 'M' AND NOT id IS NULL;

Question: Give me all IDs who have SLE disease
SQL: SELECT id FROM patient WHERE diagnosis = 'SLE';

Question: Give me everyone that has severe thrombosis
SQL: SELECT * FROM examination WHERE thrombosis = 1;

Question: How many examinations have been conducted
SQL: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM examination;
